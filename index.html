Mirage (Apex Legends)

Brief description

In Apex Legends, Mirage is known as a trickster, bamboozling his enemies with his team of decoy clones. Ever since his character introduction at the start of Apex Legends' as a game, I was drawn to his 'witty' personality and unique playstyle.

Why I like playing him

The feeling of 'bamboozling' my enemies, tricking them into shooting a holographic decoy brings me so much more joy than just simply eliminating them.

Playing Mirage forces you to rely more on your gunplay because other characters in Apex Legends have abilities that simply outclass Mirage, but I personally find this really rewarding because it means that I won with skill rather than reliance on abilities.

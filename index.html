This simple website briefly details some of my favourite videogame characters to play and why I like playing them.

About

Bryant O plays all sorts of genre of videogames, ranging from first-person shooters (FPS), role-playing games (RPGs), MOBAs, simulation games, and more.

Mirage (Apex Legends)

Brief description

In Apex Legends, Mirage is known as a trickster, bamboozling his enemies with his team of decoy clones. Ever since his character introduction at the start of Apex Legends' as a game, I was drawn to his 'witty' personality and unique playstyle.

Why I like playing him

The feeling of 'bamboozling' my enemies, tricking them into shooting a holographic decoy brings me so much more joy than just simply eliminating them.

Playing Mirage forces you to rely more on your gunplay because other characters in Apex Legends have abilities that simply outclass Mirage, but I personally find this really rewarding because it means that I won with skill rather than reliance on abilities.

Soraka (League of Legends)

Brief description

In League of Legends, Soraka is a support character who relies on her unparalleled healing and her silence crowd-control (which causes enemies to be unable to cast abilities) to protect her allies.

Why I like playing her

In League of Legends, enchanter supports like Soraka are typically priority targets for the enemy team to take down. Knowing this, I love acting as bait and an annoying harasser with my damaging/crowd-control abilities, frustrating the enemy to hopefully allow my teammates to clean up the fights and win!

Also, I love experiencing those moments when I do my all to heal my teammates just enough to survive the enemy attacks. It can sometimes seem like a thankless job - people are gonna notice the flashy takedowns over the support healing, but it's worth it to see just one teammate thank you for saving their life after the fact.

Bidoof (Pokémon)

Brief description

Bidoof is a fan-favourite Pokémon, beloved by the Pokémon community for their cute looks and endearing personality of determination

Why I like playing them

My first Pokémon game was Pokémon Diamond, where Bidoof is one of the first Pokémon you can encounter. I fell in love with Bidoof (reasons more or less found above) inspite of its lack of combat power because their simply a friend trying their best!

Bidoof has become so well loved that the Pokémon Company has dedicated July 1st as Bidoof Day!

They have even made a short film dedicated to Bidoof, called Bidoof's Big Stand
